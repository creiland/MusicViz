Instructions

Use the blue dot in the center to control the wave of the synthesizer. Right is a sine wave, left is a square wave, up is an elastic wave, down is a triangle wave. Anywhere in the middle is a combination of all of them. To place notes, click on the arc quadrants on the outside, the notes will play at the tempo, pitch, and duration determined by the slider controls below the visualization. Clicking the note multiple times sets the pan level. Notes clicked twice will turn orange and play only through the left channel, notes clicked three times will do the opposite. Clicking a fourth time will remove the note.

Explanation

The Synth Composer will allow you to create loops with various synthesized waves
