Instructions

Click visualize audio to begin playing audio and visualizing frequency data in real time. Click and drag to highlight a frequency range and apply and bandpass filter

Explanation

The Frequency Spectrum Analyser shows the decibel value of each frequency bin for the current audio. There are 4096 bins based on a Fast Fourier Tranformation applied to the audio frequency data. Clicking and dragging applies a filter to the output, reducing the volume of the audio outside the filter region.
