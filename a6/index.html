Click Visualize Audio to analyze to retrieve the BPM of the song.

Explanation

This visualization leverages an algorithm to determine the tempo of a given song. It produces 3 related data points. The bar chart on the bottom plots the peaks returned from the algorithm at their time position in the track on the X-Axis. Each bar represents a kick drum at either quarter, half, or full notes. The visualization above displays the analyzed BPM surrounded by an arc showing the current beat in a measure. The arc represents one full measure, with each quarter turn representing a quarter note.
